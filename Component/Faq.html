Device Questions

How can I connect my device to the sensor?

  1. Connect red wire into +3v to 5v port
  2. Connect black wire into GND port
  3. Connect purple wire into signal port
  4. Connect your device into your laptop via usb port

What is my IP address?

Type "localhost" or "127.0.0.1"

When I click "Scan for device", the message pops up "Device not found"

Make sure your device is correctly connected the sensor and your laptop. Also your internet connection is stable.
